About me

I am a frontend developer based in Weeze, North-Rhine-Westphalia in Germany. I have built several Web-Apps and attended an extensive Coding School, where I learned professional operating principles in programming. Feel welcome to visit my public portfolio, which includes responsive websites, a small 2d-game and a Kanban-based task manager.

My idea to enhance my programming skills grew in 2022.
I was looking for a simple app as a companion for the players of a complex boardgame called 'Mage Knight'. The main goal was to reduce the needed space on the table.
I couldn't find a appropriate app on the web and so I wanted to build one myself.
Unfortunately I only had little knowledge about html much fewer about javascript.

So I began to read much about programming and did my first steps with javascript.
In an intense 40-hours online course I learned very much of the basics by building a small functional budget book. From that time I was really hooked by the possibilities and solutions that can be achieved by web development.
In November 2022 I decided to get my knowledge to a professional level by attending Developer Akademie. I would love to bring my passion for web development into your team.
